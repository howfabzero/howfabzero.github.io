Title : Andrà tutto bene | I will be Fine | سأكون على ما يرام

Author : Dr. Essam Samir & Lama Azar | د. عصام اسمير ولما عازر

Illustrator: Hanan Kai | حنان القاعي

Publisher: Jabal Amman Publishers | جبل عمان ناشرون

ISBN : 9789923120552

Award Category : Best Production

Awarded Year : 2020

Questa storia è basata sul personaggio di Salim il bambino, che con la sua famiglia cerca rifugio in una città vicina, scappando da una situazione difficile. Dopo aver perso il padre in circostanze misteriose, Salim cerca di ricominciare una nuova vita nella nuova città, ma abituarsi al nuovo ambiente non è facile: nuova scuola, nuovo quartiere, nuovi amici. Tuttavia, la vera difficoltà risiede nella condizione psicologica di Salim e nella sua evoluzione dopo i traumi che ha vissuto. I sintomi sono insonnia, paura dei luoghi e delle persone che potrebbero ricordargli i traumi vissuti, comportamenti aggressivi a scuola e fuori, sbalzi d'umore, variazioni negative del suo livello di autostima e cambiamento della sua visione del mondo.

This book’s story is based on the character of Salim the kid, along with his family that sought refuge in a near city, due to hard conditions. After the family lost its father in mysterious circumstances, Salim attempts to start a new life in this neighboring city, but he faces difficulties getting accustomed with his new environment. Some of the difficulties stem from Salim’s need to adjust to the new school and neighborhood, and to make new friends. However, his real hardship lies in his psychological condition and its fluctuations after the traumas he experienced before. His symptoms include insomnia, fear of places and people who may remind him of an earlier trauma, aggressive behavior inside and outside the school, negative changes to his general mood, which include his self-esteem and his world perspective.

تدور احداث الكتاب حول شخصية الطفل سليم وعائلته، والتي نزحت بسبب ظروف صعبة الى مدينة مجاورة، بعد ما فقدت العائلة الأب في أوضاع غامضة. يحاول سليم أن يبدأ حياته الجديدة في هذه المدينة المجاورة، لكن تواجهه صعوبات في التأقلم مع البيئة الجديدة. بعض هذه الصعوبات ناتج عن حاجة سليم إلى التأقلم مع تكوين صداقات جديدة في المدرسة والحارة، لكن الصعوبات الحقيقية تكمن في حالة سليم النفسية، والتغيرات التي طرأت عليها بعد تعرضه لصدمات نفسية سابقة. وتشمل هذه الأعراض: صعوبة في النوم، والحاجة إلى تجنب أماكن أو أشخاص يذكرونه بالحدث الصادم، وسلوك عدواني داخل المدرسة وخارجها، علاوة على تغيرات سلبية في مزاجه العام، كنظرته إلى نفسه، وإلى العالم من حوله.
