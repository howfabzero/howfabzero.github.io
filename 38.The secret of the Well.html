Title : Il segreto del pozzo | The Secret of the Water Well | سر البئر

Author : Ali Al Qasim | علي القاسم

Illustrator: Masouma Haji Wand | معصومة حاجي وند

Publisher: Al Buragh publisher | دار البراق لثقافة الأطفال

ISBN : 9786004661843

Award Category : Silent Book Category

Awarded Year : 2019

Una storia fittizia in cui il pozzo non è un vero pozzo. ...O forse sì? Lo scoprirete attraverso i disegni di questo straordinario silent book.

This is a fictitious story where the well is not a real one. However, some kind of truth was hiding behind it, and you will find out about it through watching the drawings inside this exciting silent book.
