Title : Anemoni | Anemones | شقائق النعمان

Author : Haya Saleh | هيا صالح

Illustrator: Aly Elzini | علي الزيني

Publisher: Dar Al Yasmine | دار الياسمين للنشر والتوزيع

ISBN : 9789957682286

Award Category : Young Adult’s Book of the Year

Awarded Year : 2020

Questa storia parla del reclutamento di bambini nei conflitti armati e di come i bambini vengono adescati dai gruppi terroristici per unirsi a loro. Il protagonista della storia, Sufyan, scopre in seguito la vera natura del gruppo e riesce a scappare e a tornare dalla sua famiglia.

This story deals with recruiting children in armed conflicts, and how they are lured by terrorist groups to join them. Later, the story’s protagonist; Sufyan the kid, finds out about the reality of the group, and then manages to escape from them and return to his family.
